Self-Tape Setup Guide

Everything you need to know about cameras, lighting, sound, and backgrounds to make your child's tape look professional.

1. The Camera

You don't need a DSLR. Your phone's camera is more than enough. Use the back camera (not the selfie camera) for better quality.

  • Horizontal (Landscape) mode only.
  • Eye level with the actor.
  • Stable tripod is mandatory.

2. Lighting

Avoid overhead lights. Use natural light from a window or a simple ring light setup.

  • Light should be in front of the actor, not behind.
  • Avoid harsh shadows.

3. Background

A solid, neutral color background is best. Blue or gray works well for most casting directors.

  • No busy patterns or domestic clutter.
  • Actor should be 3 feet away from the background to avoid shadows.

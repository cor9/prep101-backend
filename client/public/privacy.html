Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2, 2025

Prep101 values your privacy and is committed to transparency in how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Summary: We collect data to provide our services, process payments, and improve our platform. We never sell your data and use industry-standard security measures to protect it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe (we don't store your card details)
  • Uploaded Content: Audition sides and scripts you upload for guide generation
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform and services
  • Analytics: Anonymous data from Google Analytics and similar tools

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To generate AI-based audition preparation guides
  • For customer support and troubleshooting
  • To send important service updates (you can opt out)
  • For security and fraud prevention

3. How We Share Data

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted vendors like Stripe (payments), Google (analytics), and AI providers (guide generation) only as necessary to operate our service
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Never Sold: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties

4. AI Processing

Uploaded PDFs may be processed by AI models (e.g., OpenAI or Anthropic) to generate your audition guides. We do not use your data to train public AI models, and your content is processed only for the purpose of providing our service.

5. Data Security

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Industry-standard security measures
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us at admin@prep101.site.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser. See our Cookie Policy for more details.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. If you are under 18, you must have parental consent to use our services.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at admin@prep101.site.
